    New Life Research Foundation, a Christian nonprofit organization, is dedicated to driving forward interdisciplinary, technology-driven research endeavors on a global scale. We firmly believe that God intends to leverage our academic and research pursuits for the purpose of spiritual revival in nations.

    In recent years, we have witnessed the deployment of individuals as instruments of influence in various nations, facilitated through this platform. God has indeed been faithful to us in mobilizing arrows in His quiver for revival in the nations.Scholarships, research initiatives, professional training, and mission endeavors have empowered numerous members to take the crucial initial steps into the nations that God has long promised them. This platform has served as a crucible for honing talents, preparing them for impactful service in the nations. Your engagement, particularly the involvement of young individuals, is indispensable to realizing this vision.

Our inclusive policy ensures that both groups can actively participate in our programs, affirming that you are valued and cherished within the New Life Research Foundation community. Our commitment to collaborative and innovative research is the bedrock of our strength, and we wholeheartedly encourage your full engagement in our programs.
