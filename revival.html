The Mustard Seed ‘NELIREF’

Revival Labor


New Life Research Foundation is a Vision that came in 2013 during the protracted 6-month Academic Staff Union of Universities (ASUU) strike. The Visioner (Joseph Luper Tsenum) spent the six-month strike seeking the Lord to discover his purpose for living. It was then that he read a book written by Bro. Gbile Akanni titled ‘The Journey into Your Vision’. This brought insight to him and the subsequent vision to form New Life Research to mobilize God’s children into different nations for revival using academics, research, and scholarships. It is worthy of note that this name New Life Research was received in 2013 as well.

New Life Research Foundation is for those who have this new life in Christ whom the Lord will be using in different nations for revival using academics and research. Jer. 1:1-19, 51:20-24 is the anchor scripture for New Life Research Foundation as the Lord promised to use anyone He wills to bring down revival in the nations.

On October 23, 2017, the Lord came again speaking to our brother on the need to start taking the ‘initiation step’ into the nations. He created a page on Facebook on that day and called few brethren together whom they prayed and started the work. While few people believed in the Vision, some did not believe in it from the onset (probably because it was a mustard seed and himself was not yet into the nations he spoke to them about). In the same 2017, few members he was working with got scholarships and traveled to Europe while he was still in Nigeria.

After applying for a scholarship in Europe in 2018 and securing it in 2019, he traveled to Russia for his Masters program and by then, more people believed in the Vision (The mustard seed NELIREF). New Life Research Foundation was formally launched on November 22, 2019

The mustard seed ‘NELIREF’ was incorporated in Nigeria as a non-profit Christian Academic/Research organization on March 2, 2022 and in Georgia, United States on May 17, 2023. New Life Research Foundation now has more than 15, 000 followers on social media platforms (including its partners). Under this time, 100s of NELIREF members secured scholarships in different nations and now set to start its Pioneer Research Cohort, Symposiums and Annual Conference. Both undergraduate and graduate scholarship applicants are mentored to become what God wants to use them in the nations to achieve.

Together with her partners such as The United Pentecostal Church (UPCI), Adullam Calvary Mission, Makurdi Christian Youth Summit, Ngunan Addingi Mentorship Program, The Moscow Goodnews Church and other research labs in North America, NELIREF is dedicated to counseling and mentoring its members to become sharp arrows in God’s quiver in the nations. 

Indeed, the Lord can use anything, the mustard seed NELIREF is set to becoming a big umbrella where both believers in Jesus and unbelievers can come to drink from the living water and discover their purpose for living. Our Vision, aims and objectives can be gotten on our banner for more elaboration.
